# DEVICE LIMITATIONS: THE UNDERESTIMATED FACTOR IN 5G THROUGHPUT

We often blame the network.
But sometimes…
The limitation is the device.
Not all 5G devices are equal.
And that directly impacts throughput.

* Device category defines maximum supported bandwidth, MIMO layers, and modulation. 
* Thermal constraints can reduce performance during sustained high throughput sessions. 
* UE capability limits aggregation combinations and feature support. 
* Real-world performance depends on how efficiently the device processes and utilizes resources. 

This creates a hidden variability.
Same network. Same location.
Different device… completely different experience.

So when analyzing throughput:
It’s not just about the network.
It’s about the ecosystem.
And ignoring device capability leads to wrong conclusions.
